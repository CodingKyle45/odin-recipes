Buffalo Wing Sauce

Buffalo Wing Sauce a combination of hot sauce mixed with melted butter or butter flavorings.

It's therefore a toned down version of hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup hot pepper sauce
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 tbs white vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a pot over medium heat
  2. Bring to simmer while stirring with a whisk
  3. As soon as it bubbles on sides of pot remove from heat
  4. Stir with whisk and set aside for use
